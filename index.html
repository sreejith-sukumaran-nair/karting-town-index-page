Welcome

Located at the heart of Sharjah and Northern Emirates, the new CIK/FIA standard go-karting track is a true adrenaline rush for its customers. With its six unique track configurations, Karting Town-Sharjah offers fast flowing straights, highly technical corners, cambers with a unique banking corner and elevation changes.

About US

We are a modern Go Karting and Multi-Entertainment Facility located at the Heart of Sharjah and Northern Emirates A sate of the art facility catering both local and international clients specialized in arrive and drive for both Adults and Kids. Offering supreme prospects towards corporate conferences, group activities, team building and track hires Karting Town is the newest landmark of Sharjah-UAE with its unique Food Truck Park and hangout zone for 7 years and above.
